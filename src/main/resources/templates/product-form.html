Primitivo Luccarelli IGT Puglia

The Primitivo Puglia from the wine-growing region of Puglia offers a dense colour in the glass. The first nose of the Primitivo Puglia reveals notes of fruit bread, dried fruit and prunes. The fruity aspects of the bouquet are joined by more fruity-balsamic nuances. This red from Luccarelli is ideal for all wine connoisseurs who like as little residual sugar as possible in their wine. At the same time, however, it never appears meager or brittle, which is absolutely not a matter of course for a wine in the entry-level range. Due to the moderate fruit acidity, the Primitivo flatters with a pleasing mouthfeel, without lacking juicy liveliness at the same time.

Food recommendation for the Primitivo Puglia from Luccarelli

This Italian is best enjoyed at a temperature of 15 - 18°C. It is perfect as an accompaniment to roasted calf's liver with apples, onions and balsamic vinegar sauce, leek tortilla or leek soup.
